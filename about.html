ABOUT US

ABOUT US:
Established in 2021, Educate kids’ is a non-profit that focusses on mobilising communities for kids’ education in India’s rural and educationally backward areas. Strongly aligned with the ‘Right to Education Act’ or the ‘Samagra Siksha’, Educate kids is committed to the Government’s vision to improve access to primary education for children, especially youngs. Educate students currently operates successfully in over 180+ villages in Gujarat. By leveraging the Government’s existing investment in schools and by engaging with a huge base of community volunteers, Educate students helps to identify, enrol and retain out-of-school students and to improve foundational skills in literacy and numeracy for all children (both girls and boys). This helps deliver measurable results to a large number of children and avoids parallel delivery of services.
VISION:
We aim to achieve behavioural, social and economic transformation for all students towards an India where all children have equal opportunities to access quality education.
MISSION:
We leverage existing community and Government resources to ensure that all students are in school and learning well.
GOAL:
Our goal is to improve access and quality of education for over 1 million children cumulatively by 2024.
